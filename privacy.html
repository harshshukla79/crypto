Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Crypto Help Desk collects, uses, and protects your information. We provide independent crypto assistance and follow strict privacy and safety standards.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Seed phrases
  • Private keys
  • Passwords or PINs
  • Two-factor authentication codes

We will never request or store this information. If anyone asks for it, do not share it.

Information We May Collect

  • Your phone number (only if you call us)
  • Basic details you choose to share during the call
  • Email address (only if you email us directly)

We collect the minimum information necessary to assist you.

How We Use Your Information

  • To return your call or email
  • To provide customer support
  • To improve service quality

Data Protection & Security

We use secure systems to protect communication. No sensitive wallet information is stored, logged, or transmitted.

Cookies

Our site uses minimal cookies to improve user experience and website performance. You may accept or decline cookies using your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

We do not sell or share your information with third parties. Analytics tools may collect general anonymized usage data.
